# Reusable Respiratory Home Care Block

Use as a shared comfort-care block for respiratory discharge instructions when clinically appropriate.

Applicable families:

- Pneumonia
- Viral URI
- Bronchitis-like illness
- Asthma flare with cough/congestion
- COPD flare with cough/congestion

Patient-facing concepts:

- Warm liquids such as tea with honey, broth, or warm water with lemon can soothe cough or sore throat and help hydration.
- Steam from a hot shower or sitting in a steamy bathroom for 10-15 minutes can loosen mucus and make coughing more productive.
- Sleeping with the head raised can reduce nighttime coughing and help secretions drain.
- A cool-mist humidifier can keep airways from drying out, especially in winter or air-conditioned rooms.
- Honey can help cough for patients old enough to use it safely.

Policy:

- Put these in `WHAT TO DO AT HOME`, not `MEDICATIONS`.
- Treat these as comfort measures, not replacement treatment.
- Do not use honey guidance in infants.
- Keep the wording practical and short.
