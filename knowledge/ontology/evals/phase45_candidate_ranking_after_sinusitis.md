# Phase 45 Candidate Ranking After Sinusitis

Status: complete.

The expansion gate reopened after `acute_sinusitis_supportive_care` promotion.

Recommendation: build `conjunctivitis_uncomplicated_no_contact_lens` next as a draft only.

Why this candidate:

- Common ED discharge phenotype.
- Clear source support from CDC and MedlinePlus.
- Good patient-education value around hygiene, contagiousness, and return precautions.
- Safer than syncope, palpitations, low-risk chest pain, biliary colic, or hyperglycemia.

Main risks:

- Contact lens keratitis.
- Eye trauma or foreign body.
- Vision loss.
- Severe pain or photophobia.
- Herpes or zoster eye disease.
- Periorbital or orbital cellulitis.
- Chemical exposure.

Decision:

Proceed to source cards, draft boundaries, and runtime cases. Do not promote conjunctivitis in this phase cycle.
