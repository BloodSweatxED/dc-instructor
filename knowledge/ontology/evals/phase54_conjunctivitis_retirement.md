# Phase 54 Conjunctivitis Retirement

Decision: retire `conjunctivitis_uncomplicated_no_contact_lens` from ontology runtime promotion.

Runtime status:

- Phenotype status: `retired`.
- Review status: `retired`.
- Runtime mode: `retired_product_layer_fallback_only`.
- Classifier may still recognize the phenotype for telemetry and fallback explanation, but it must not produce ontology-mode patient instructions.

Reason:

Eye complaints have too much false-reassurance risk for promotion without explicit clinician review. Contact lens use, corneal disease, chemical injury, glaucoma mimic language, trauma, vision change, photophobia, herpes/zoster eye disease, cellulitis concern, immunocompromise, and pregnancy all remain hard blockers.

Outcome: expansion can reopen after conjunctivitis because it is no longer an active draft.
