# Phase 41 Acute Sinusitis Clinician Review

Target phenotype: `acute_sinusitis_supportive_care`.

Decision: approve for narrow reviewed runtime use as supportive-care sinusitis without an antibiotic plan.

Review constraints:

- Keep supportive-care wording.
- Do not say antibiotics are never needed.
- Do not provide medication names, doses, schedules, or durations.
- Do not promise exact symptom duration.
- Do not include watchful waiting as an explicit treatment policy in this phenotype.
- Block antibiotic-prescribed sinusitis and severe bacterial-feature language.
- Block orbital, intracranial, dental, facial trauma, sepsis, pregnancy, frailty, immunocompromise, unstable vitals, chronic, or recurrent pathways.

Promotion requirement: Phase 38 and Phase 39 cases must pass before promotion.
