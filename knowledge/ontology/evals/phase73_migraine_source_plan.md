# Phase 73 Migraine Source-First Plan

Target phenotype: `migraine_improved_discharge`.

Source cards:

- `medlineplus.migraine`
- `ninds.headache`

Inclusion:

- Adult with clinician diagnosis or discharge impression of migraine that improved after ED treatment.
- No thunderclap onset, neurologic deficit, infection concern, head trauma, pregnancy, immunocompromise, unstable vitals, or atypical high-risk headache features documented.

Exclusion:

- Thunderclap or worst headache, subarachnoid hemorrhage concern, stroke/TIA concern, new neurologic deficit, seizure, or altered mental status.
- Fever with headache, meningitis/encephalitis concern, pregnancy, immunocompromise, head trauma, age over 50 with new headache, or unstable vital signs.
- Uncontrolled vomiting, specialist-directed neurology plan, or headache not improved enough for discharge.

Source limitations:

MedlinePlus supports migraine education. NINDS supports headache warning concepts. Neither source can replace clinician judgment that secondary headache has been excluded.
