# Phase 57 Otitis Externa Source-First Plan

Target phenotype: `otitis_externa_no_mastoiditis`.

Source cards:

- `cdc.swimmers_ear`
- `medlineplus.swimmers_ear`

Inclusion:

- Adult with clinician diagnosis or discharge impression of uncomplicated otitis externa or swimmer's ear.
- No mastoiditis, malignant otitis externa, middle-ear complication, ear trauma, foreign body, acute hearing loss, facial weakness, or systemic illness concern documented.

Exclusion:

- Mastoiditis, deep ear infection, skull base osteomyelitis, or malignant otitis externa concern.
- Diabetes, immunocompromised host, severe systemic illness, unstable vitals, or fever needing separate planning.
- Perforated eardrum, tympanostomy tube, ear trauma, ear foreign body, acute hearing loss, vertigo, facial weakness, or specialist-directed ENT plan.

Must-not-miss:

- Mastoiditis.
- Malignant otitis externa.
- Skull base osteomyelitis.
- Perforated tympanic membrane.
- Ear foreign body.
- Sudden sensorineural hearing loss.

Source limitations:

CDC mainly supports prevention and patient education. MedlinePlus supports symptoms, treatment concepts, and escalation signals. Neither source alone supports ED risk stratification, so runtime use must remain draft-only until clinician review.
