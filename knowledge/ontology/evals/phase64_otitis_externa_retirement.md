# Phase 64 Otitis Externa Retirement

Decision: retire `otitis_externa_no_mastoiditis` from ontology runtime promotion.

Runtime status:

- Phenotype status: `retired`.
- Review status: `retired`.
- Runtime mode: `retired_product_layer_fallback_only`.

Reason:

Otitis externa is sourceable and probably salvageable, but promotion needs explicit approval of mastoiditis, malignant otitis externa, perforation or tube, trauma or foreign body, acute hearing loss, facial weakness, diabetes, immunocompromise, fever, and unstable-vitals boundaries.
