# Phase 23 Abdominal Pain Narrowing

Status: narrowed draft; not promoted.

## Decision

Keep the existing draft ID for lineage, but narrow the clinical target:

`abdominal_pain_nonspecific_reassuring_workup` now means abdominal pain recheck guidance after a reassuring ED evaluation.

It does not mean nonspecific abdominal pain is safe. It does not create reviewed ontology mode.

## New Boundary

The draft can support:

- Reassuring ED evaluation today.
- Symptoms can evolve after discharge.
- Clear return precautions.
- Follow-up or recheck instructions.

It cannot support:

- Static reassurance that abdominal pain is benign.
- Broad undifferentiated abdominal pain discharge.
- Pregnancy-related abdominal pain.
- Localized or worsening pain concerning for surgical abdomen.
- Uncontrolled pain or vomiting.
- Fever, GI bleeding, sepsis concern, frailty, immunocompromise, or unreliable follow-up.

## Gate Result

Phase 23 keeps expansion blocked. Stress-test coverage now exists, but clinician review and promotion have not happened.

## Next Phase

Phase 24 should create the clinician review packet for this narrowed abdominal-pain draft and decide whether to promote it or retire it.
