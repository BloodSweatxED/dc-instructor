# Phase 72 Candidate Ranking

Chosen candidate: `migraine_improved_discharge`.

Reason:

Migraine is a useful ED discharge target and appears in generator stress outputs, but the secondary-headache boundary is the main risk. It should be built as draft only with hard blockers for thunderclap headache, neurologic deficit, meningitis/CNS infection concern, head trauma, pregnancy, immunocompromise, age over 50 with new headache, uncontrolled vomiting, and unstable vitals.
