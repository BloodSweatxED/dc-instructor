# Phase 49 Conjunctivitis Review Packet

Target phenotype: `conjunctivitis_uncomplicated_no_contact_lens`.

Decision: keep as draft. Do not promote yet.

Reason:

The source-supported hygiene and return-precaution content is useful, but eye complaints carry a high penalty for false reassurance. Contact lens use, corneal injury, keratitis, uveitis, glaucoma, herpes/zoster eye disease, and orbital cellulitis must stay outside the phenotype until clinician review confirms the boundary.

Review needs:

- Confirm no contact lens users enter ontology mode.
- Confirm no broad red-eye fallback.
- Confirm no antibiotic eye-drop instructions are inferred.
- Confirm return precautions are strong enough for pain, photophobia, vision change, swelling, trauma, and chemical exposure.
