WHAT TO DO AT HOME:

MEDICATIONS:

RETURN TO ED IF:
- New numbness, weakness, blue or pale toes, or severe worsening pain.

Follow up with primary care, orthopedics, urgent care, or the ED if symptoms are not improving or your clinician told you to recheck.
