DIAGNOSIS:
Your symptoms fit blepharitis, irritation and inflammation of the eyelids.

WHAT WE FOUND:
Your clinician diagnosed uncomplicated blepharitis. We did not find vision changes, severe eye pain, eye injury, or signs of a deeper infection around the eye today.

WHAT TO DO AT HOME:
- Use warm compresses as your clinician instructed.
- Clean the eyelid edges gently as instructed.
- Avoid eye makeup and contact lenses until your clinician says they are safe.

MEDICATIONS:
- Use eye medicine only if it was prescribed.
- Do not put non-eye medicines or harsh products in the eye.

RETURN TO ED IF:
- Vision change, severe eye pain, light sensitivity, or pain with eye movement.
- Fever, swelling spreading around the eye, the eye bulges, or you cannot open the eyelid.
- Symptoms get much worse or do not improve with the plan you were given.

FOLLOW UP:
Follow up with primary care, urgent care, or eye care if symptoms are not improving, and sooner if any red flag develops.
